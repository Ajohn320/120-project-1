Coming up Soon

Coming up soon, Coming soon.
Your throat felt thick with dryness as you swallowed.
Those words echoed inside of your hollow chest.
Mimicking your heart beat like some cruel mockery.
The reminder was constant and cursed.
Sending waves of dread through you as each hour and each day passed by.
You had a the weight of the world on your shoulders.
It was suffocating you.
You start to wish you were a child again.
You didn't know what you were lacking until you grew older.
But now your like a fish out of water.
Flopping around desperately and stubbornly for anything that felt like home.
The words echo in your chest like a warning.
So you hide and wait for the inevitable.
Maybe then you’ll learn what you need.
So enjoy your 15 minutes
...it's coming soon.
